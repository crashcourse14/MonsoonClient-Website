About

Monsoon Client. Monsoon doesn't mean heavy rain. It really just means a big shift in wind... But in this case it's a Eaglercraft 1.8 client! It's built to improve player experience, to be FPS friendly, and to be very customizable. It's currently still In development; We're shooting for at LEAST 30 working mods. Once that's achieve we MIGHT release it. If not we'll shoot for 50+ mods.
