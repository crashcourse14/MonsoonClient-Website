About

Monsoon doesn't mean heavy rain. It really just means a big shift in wind... But in this case, it's an Eaglercraft 1.8 client! It's built to improve player experience, to be FPS friendly, and to be highly customizable. It's currently still in development; we're shooting for at LEAST 30 working mods. Once that's achieved, we MIGHT release it. If not, we'll shoot for 50+ mods.
